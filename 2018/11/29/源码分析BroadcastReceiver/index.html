源码分析BroadcastReceiver

源码分析BroadcastReceiver

BroadcastReceiver注册：

1 ContextWrapper.registerReceiver()

2 ContextImpl.registerReceiver()

3 ContextImpl.registerReceiverInternal()做一些参数的检查，scheduler的赋值，通过ActivityManager.getService()获取AMS代理，调用AMS registerReceiver

4 AMS registerReceiver() 注册广播的就是将InnerReceiver对象，IntentFilter对象封装在类BroadcastFilter中，并最终将BroadcastFilter对象存储在变量mReceiverResolver中。

5 IntentFilter的match方法

广播的发送：

1 ContextWrapper.sendBroadcast()

2 ContextImpl.sendBroadcast() ActivityManager.getService()获取AMS代理并调用broadcastIntent()

3 AMS broadcastIntent 检查flag并调用broadcastIntentLocked

4 AMS broadcastIntentLocked

设置flag为FLAG_EXCLUDE_STOPPED_PACKAGES时，广播不会发送给已经停止的应用。

调用IntentResolver$queryIntent方法用于获取与该intent相匹配的广播接受者，返回的是一个List集合，因此变量registeredReceivers是一个List类型变量。

5 BroadcastQueue scheduleBroadcastsLocked 广播分发

6 handleMSG processNextBroadcast

7 BroadcastQueue.processNextBroadcast() -> processNextBroadcastLocked

8 BroadcastQueue.processNextBroadcastLocked 取出mParallelBroadcasts的接受者并遍历发送

9 BroadcastQueue.deliverToRegisteredReceiverLocked

10 BroadcastQueue.performReceiveLocked app.thread返回IApplicationThread接口的代理对象，最终会调用ApplicationThread$scheduleRegisteredReceiver方法

11 ActivityThread.ApplicationThread.scheduleRegisteredReceiver

变量receiver是一个IIntentReceiver类型的对象，在注册广播的流程中提到过IIntentReceiver，它的具体实现类是LoadedApk.ReceiverDispatcher.InnerReceiver。其实InnerReceiver是一个Binder接口，封装BroadcastReceiver对象后，使组件对象可以在进程间传递。

12 LoadedApk.ReceiverDispatcher.InnerReceiver scheduleRegisteredReceiver

变量mActivityThread是ActivityThread$H类型的对象，它继承了Handler，接受广播的操作是在Handler所在的线程中执行，也就是在ActivityThread主线程中执行。

13 LoadedApk.ReceiverDispatcher.Args

回调BroadcastReceiver.onReceive方法，于是前面注册的广播接受者，就接收到发送的广播了
